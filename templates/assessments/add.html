Assessment Guidelines
Basic Measurements
  • Weight: Record in kilograms (kg)
  • Height: Record in centimeters (cm)
  • Take measurements at the same time of day for consistency
Body Composition
  • Body Fat: Use body fat scale or calipers (%)
  • Muscle Mass: Total muscle mass in kg
  • Consider using DEXA scan for accuracy
Body Measurements
  • Chest: Measure at nipple level
  • Waist: Measure at narrowest point
  • Hips: Measure at widest point
  • Arm: Measure at largest point of bicep
  • Thigh: Measure at largest point
  • Neck: Measure just below Adam's apple
Photo Guidelines
  • Take photos in good lighting
  • Use consistent poses and angles
  • Front, side, and back views recommended
  • Wear minimal, form-fitting clothing
Tip: Regular assessments help evaluate changes and adjust training programs effectively.
